Rocky Reifenstuhl

"Dad, husband, brother, freezer-geezer, cyclist, geologist, Alaskan, friend and general pain-in-the-ass."

Rocky in the Sadlerochit Mountains

General Events

  • 1952 - Born and grew up in rural New York
  • 1976 - Graduated from West Virginia University with a BA in Geology
  • 1977 - Met his wife Gail Koepf
  • 1981 - Graduated from UAF with a MS in Geology
  • 2009 - Retired from the Alaska Division of Geological & Geophyscal Surveys (DGGS) after 27 years
  • Jan 31st, 2014 - Rocky passes away at 61 after dealing with cardiac issues and waiting for a heart transplant

Professional Life

  • Senior Author on 10 geologic maps and Contributing Author to an additional 38 maps
  • Lead Author for 29 reports and technical papers and co-author on an additional 27
  • Served as President and Board Member of the Alaska Geological Society
  • Served as Adjunct Professor at University of Alaska Fairbanks
  • Fossil Palaeozygopleura (Rhenozyga) reifenstulhli named in his honor
